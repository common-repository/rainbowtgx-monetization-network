RainbowTgx inserts native advertisement widgets to your site or blog to let you add additional revenue streams to your online monetization strategy.

== Description ==

RainbowTgx is an Italian Native Monetization Network aimed at inserting contextual native advertisement widgets to your site or blog and lets you choose the widget position in your content.

Major features in RainbowTgx include:

* Automatically insert RainbowTgx activation script to all the pages of your site.
* Add RainbowTgx shortlink to allow you place RainbowTgx native advertisement anywhere in your blog.
* Add RainbowTgx widget to allow you place RainbowTgx native advertisement in any widget area in your blog.
* Option to add RainbowTgx native advertisement before your content and/or after your content in every post and page.

PS: You'll need a RainbowTgx account and Customer ID [rainbowtgx.com CID key](http://www.rainbowtgx.com/) to use it. 
You can get an account by submitting you site to http://www.rainbowtgx.com/it/contatti-rainbowtgx/ .

== Installation ==

Upload the RainbowTgx plugin to your blog, Activate it, choose the widget position then enter your [rainbowtgx.com CID key](http://www.rainbowtgx.com/).

1, 2, 3: You're done!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Come posso attivare RainbowTgx sul mio sito? =

E’ sufficiente compilare il modulo online disponibile all’indirizzo http://www.rainbowtgx.com/it/contatti-rainbowtgx/ per richiedere di entrare nel network RainbowTgx.

= Dove viene posizionato RainbowTgx? =

Il plugin permette di posizionare fino a 3 spazi RainbowTgx per pagina:
- prima del contenuto in pagine e post
- dopo il contenuto in pagine e post
- all’interno di un widget
